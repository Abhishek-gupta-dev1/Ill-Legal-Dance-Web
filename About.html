ILL_LEGALS

How it started?

Initially we never thought of forming a club or crew. Some of us performed together in some places and got motivation to form a permanent team then we searched for a place , named our team and after sometime we started to explore in different art forms. People started to love our work and asked us to teach them. That's how the scenario about teaching all the art forms started in offline and virtual mode. We won many district level competitions and awards in dancing and designing.

How we named it?

We all very curious to give an appealing and different name to our club. After a long discussiona and cancelling out many names, we named it "ILL_LEGALS' which means the each and every member of our team is legally ill for thier passion and dream.
This is how our beautiful club was formed.
